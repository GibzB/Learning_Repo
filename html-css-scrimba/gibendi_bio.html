Peter Gibendi Biography

The Enviromentalist, The Champion, The Chemist

He was the true definition of focus and determination, a great story teller, beating the odds and succeeding in his life in his own way.

He was born in 1963 in a small village; Shiru in Vihiga County, Kenya. He was the only son in a family of 10. Being raised by his grandfather and grandmother He attended his primary school in the same village and later joined a provincial school for his secondary education. He later joined the University of Nairobi for his undergraduate studies in Chemistry. He graduated in 2014 with a Second Class Honours (Upper Division). He later joined the same university for his masters degree in Analytical Chemistry. He graduated in 2018 with a Second Class Honours (Upper Division).

He was a great sportsman, playing football and volleyball. He was also a great athlete, running 100m, 200m and 400m. He was also a great swimmer, swimming in the rivers and lakes in his village. He was also a great boxer, winning many boxing matches in his village.

Education

  • Primary School: Shiru Primary School
  • Secondary School: St. Mary's Yala High School
  • Undergraduate: University of Nairobi
  • Masters: University of Nairobi
